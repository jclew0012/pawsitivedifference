OUR GOAL

The primary goal of PAWsitive Difference is to rescue, foster, and adopt out dogs and cats from families that are unable to continue to care for them. We also rescue many pets that have been abandoned or are listed on the kill list of local shelters and help provide them homes with loving families.

TAKE ACTION

We took in 130 cats and 802 dogs in 2025. Help us save more in 2026!
